MY STORY


Hi, my name is

Angela Jovanovska.

I was born on August 22th, 2003, in Kumanovo, Macedonia. I went to Kristijan Karposh, middle school, and later finished high school in Gimnazija “Goce Delcev”, Kumanovo. Currently I am attending the "Faculty of Computer science and engineering" at "Ss. Cyril and Methodius" University in Skopje. I am 19 years old and an only child. I like to make people around me feel loved and appreciated, I am also a great listener and my friends say that I give the best advice. That's the most important to me, because no matter what you or your life looks like, or what career you have, the amount of kindness that you give off to the world, and your closest is really what shapes you as a person.
